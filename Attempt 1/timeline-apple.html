Apple Inc. Timeline

By Abdullah Ibna Aamir

Timeline: History of Apple & Their Products

1976: Apple is founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
1976: Apple I computer is released.
1977: Apple II launches, becoming a huge success.
1984: Macintosh is introduced, the first mass-market personal computer with a GUI.
1998: iMac G3 is released, known for its colorful design.
2001: First iPod is launched, revolutionizing music.
2007: iPhone is introduced, changing the smartphone industry.
2010: iPad is released, creating a new category of devices.
2015: Apple Watch launches, entering the wearable market.
2020: Apple introduces Macs with its own M1 silicon chips.
2024: Apple Vision Pro is announced, entering spatial computing.
